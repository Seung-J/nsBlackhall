  • A model distinguished for ultra-high sensitivity, providing excellent capability to probe in vast sea areas

    This model is a Korean indigenous flagship model, ideal for the 3 types of traditional fishing techniques of Korea. It was built to the specifications enabling anglers to fish in coastal waters and even in deep sea. The blank adopted the CTX to provide smooth action and high resilience while the targeted fish is landed, so that anglers can subdue the struggling fish easily. The excellent stiffness of blank and highly elastic rod tip allow the angler to detect vibration transmitted by sinker, thus making it very easy to find the targeted fish at the deflected point in river or sea floor.

  • B-652M (GRIP 400mm)

    This model provides anglers with the capability to respond to delicate situations with finesse. The rod is ideal for lightweight fishing tackle, and the strong blank allows anglers to experience the thrilling sensation when fish takes the bite. The blank, which adopted CTX, doubled the strength, resilience and sensitivity. In addition, Fuji guide was adopted to increase adaptability to the seawaters.

  • B-672MH (GRIP 400mm)

    This model is the most basic for halibut lure fishing and can be used for fishing in all points including shallow waters, river channel, fish reef, deep waters, etc. The tip of rod was finished with the CTX, allowing the angle to detect even the lightest bite of fish.

  • B-692H (GRIP 400mm)

    This model is ideal for the targeted fish in deep waters. The rod tip was designed to increase convenience in using heavy sinkers. The tip, which adopted the CTX, provides ultra-high sensitivity and excellent strength. The ultra-sensitive tip increased the transmission of vibration to the hand from even the slightest sign of bites of targeted fish, while the butt maintains the power strong enough to fight and land any large fish.
